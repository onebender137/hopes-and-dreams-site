ALKALOID PROFILE: MITRAGYNA SPECIOSA
1. Mitragynine (MG): 
   - Makes up ~66% of total alkaloid content.
   - Primary Stimulant: At low doses (1-5g), it acts as an adrenergic agonist, increasing focus and physical energy.
   - Mechanism: Binds to mu-opioid receptors but acts as a G-protein biased agonist (low risk of respiratory depression compared to traditional opioids).

2. 7-Hydroxymitragynine (7-OH):
   - Minor constituent (<2% in raw leaf) but 13-46x more potent than morphine.
   - Primary Analgesic: Responsible for the "Sedative" mode at high doses (5-15g).
   - Warning: Semi-synthetic extracts high in 7-OH are much higher risk for physical dependence.

BIOHACKING PROTOCOL:
- The "Focus" Mode: 1g to 2g of White or Green vein.
- The "Recovery" Mode: 4g to 6g of Red vein.
- Tolerance Ceiling: Never exceed 15g in 24 hours to avoid 'The Wobbles' (vestibular system disruption).
